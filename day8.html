Osaka Aquarium

Near Universal Studios, is Osaka Aquarium - one of Japan's most spectacular aquariums. It homes various forms of sea life that inhabit the pacific ocean. The central tank houses whale sharks as seen in the photo below.

Cup Noodles Museum

I highly recommend the Cup Noodles Museum which is sure to be a blast. The museum has various exhibitions and attractions, including:

- Chicken Ramen Factory: You can make Chicken Ramen by hand, going through the entire process of kneading, spreading, and steaming the wheat flour, and then drying it. After you finish, you get to take home your freshly made noodles. TRAVEL TIP: make your reservation in advance. You can make a reservation by phone (072-751-0825) between the house of 10:00 and 16:00 (local time). You can also make a reservation online but the online system is only in Japanese.

- My CUPNOODLES Factory: The main attraction. Here you can create your own fully customized CUPNOODLES package. You will design the cup, select your favorite soup and toppings (from an array of choices). There are over 5,000 possible combitions. Reservations are not needed.

- Exhibition of Instant Noodles: This exhibit depicts the worldwide popularization of instant noodles and shows annual consumption figures of various countries and CUPNOODLES packages from around the world.

- Instant Noodles Tunnel: This exhibit displays the instant noodles lineup, housing over 800 different packages of instant noodles.

Dontonbori

Then head over to Dontonbori to spend your night. Dontonbori street runs parrallel to the Dontonbori cancal and is a popular shopping and entertainment district. It is also a food destination. At night, it is lit by countless neon lights and mechanized signs.
