Day 7 | Nara

Miyakoji Rapid Train

Pack your bags were leaving Kyoto. Take the Miyakoji rapid trains (which operate every 30 minutes) between Kyoto Station and JR Nara Station. The one way trip takes 45 minutes, is covered your JR Pass.

Nara Koen Park

After arriving in Nara, head over to Nara Koen Park. The park is home to hundreds of freely roaming deer. The deer are considered to be messengers of the gods by the Shinto. You canb buy deer crackers (which are for sale around the park) if you want to try feeding the deer.

Todaiji Temple

Then head over to Todaiji Temple - one of Japan's most famous temples and a landmark of Nara. The temple was constructed to be the head temple of all Buddhist temples of Japan and became so powerful that the capital of Japan was moved from Nara in order to lower the temple's influence on the government.

JR Kanjo-Yamatoji

That concludes your day in Nara. From the JR Nara Station take the JR Kanjo-Yamatoji Line to JR Osaka Station. You will be in Osaka for Day 8 so grab a hotel for the night.
