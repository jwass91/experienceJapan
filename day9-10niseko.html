Day 9-10 | Niseko

KIX to CTS

Take a morning flight from Osaka’s Kansai International Airport (KIX) to Sapporo’s New Chitose Airport (CTS). The flight is about 2 hours.

Sapporo to Niseko

Then from New Chitose Airport (CTS) take a shuttle bus or train to Niseko.

Mount Niseko-Annupuri

Niseko's ski resorts are all on Mount Niseko-Annupuri. Niseko is known for having tons of powder snow so its a great place to ski and/or snowboard. There are three major resorts and several smaller resorts, but the biggest resort is Grand Hirafu, which has over a dozen lifts. Grand Hirafu also has a nice town (Hirafu Town) at its base, which is packed with hotels, resturants, and shops. TRAVEL TIP: If you are a big skier, and want to ski all three resorts, you should get a special combination ticket, called the Niseko All Mountain Pass, that gives access to all three resorts.
