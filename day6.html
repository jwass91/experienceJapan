Day 6 | Kyoto

Nishiki Market

Known as "Kyoto's Kitchen", this lively market specializes in all things food related - including like fresh fish, produce, and cookware.

Option 1

Kiyomizudera Temple

Kiyomizudera Temple Kiyomizudera is one of the most celebrated temples of Japan. The Japanese name of the temple 清水寺, literally "Pure Water Temple". The temple is on the site of the Otowa Waterfall in east of Kyoto and is named after the fall's pure waters. You will use cups attached to long poles to drink from one of the three separate streams.

Option 2

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Fushimi Inari Shrine is an important Shinto shrine in southern Kyoto. It is famous for its thousands of torii gates, which cover the trails behind its main buildings.
