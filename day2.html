Day 2 | Tokyo

Yanaka Ginza Street

Head over to Yanaka, one of the few districts that still has an old town ambience reminiscent of Tokyo from past decades. Yanaka Ginza Street is full of shops selling goods ranging from groceries to toys.

Yanaka Cemetery

Head over to Yanaka Cemetery, where many of the tombs are elaborately decorated and nicely landscaped. The grave of the last shogun of the Edo Period is in this cemetery.

Ueno

From Yanaka take a subway to Ueno to see some parks and museums. After visitng Ueno Park, you can visit the Ueno Zoo, the National Museum of Nature and Science, and/or the Tokyo Metropolitan Art Museum.

Tokyo Imperial Palace

From Ueno head over to the Tokyo Imperial Palace (via train) to see a large park area surrounded by moats and stone walls (the former site of the Edo Castle).
