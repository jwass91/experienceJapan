Tsukiji Fish Market

Tyoko Day 3


Take an early morning visit to Tsukiji market, the world’s biggest and busiest fish markets

TRAVEL TIP: If you’re an early bird and want to see something remarkable try to visit the tuna auction. The number of visitors to the tuna auction is limited to 120 per day, so you have to apply at the Osakana Fukyu Center (Fish Information Center) at the Kachidoki Gate, starting from 5:00am (or earlier on busy days) on a first-come, first-serve basis.

Otherwise just visit the outer market to find food related goods and fresh seafood in consumer quantities. I recommend grabbing a sushi breakfast/lunch nearby the market.
