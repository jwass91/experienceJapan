Day 10 | Miyajima

Miyajima Island Ferry

Miyajima is a small island less than an hour outside of Hiroshima. To get to Miyajima you will take a train and a ferry. Using your JR Pass, take the JR Sanyo Line from Hiroshima Station to Miyajimaguchi Station (25 minutes). From Miyajimaguchi Station, take a short walk to the ferry pier and then take a ferry to Miyajima island. There are two different ferries, but you should take the JR ferry because it is covered by your JR Pass.

Itsukushima Shrine

Itsukushima Shrine is the island's most famed landmark. In fact, the name Miyajima literally means "shrine island". The shrine is known for its famous "floating" torii gate which appear to float in the sea during high tide.

Optional

Mount Misen

A ten minute walk from the Itsukushima Shrine, you will frind the ropeway station to take you up to Mount Misen. The ride up takes about 20 minutes. There are then three hiking trails leading up to the very top, but they are fiarly steep. There are spectular views from the peak of Mount Misen.
